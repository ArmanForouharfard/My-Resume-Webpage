List of the Global projects

The project HAVARD is a satellite that, by connecting to a robotic laboratory, can carry out dangerous experiments in zero-gravity. This satellite is also equipped with an artificial intelligence that has 2 main goals:
1- Accessing and conducting experiments using the available ligaments in the satellite laboratory department.
2- Identifying the spreading diseases and scanning all the existing life effects caused by that disease and finding a solution or a treatment to eliminate that disease.

Project SUNSHINE is a process for traveling to the planets of the solar system,
With SUNSHINE armor reinforced with hexagonal solar panels and satellites equipped with solar panels, this trip can be made possible by placing each of these satellites in all planets to have enough energy to return.
